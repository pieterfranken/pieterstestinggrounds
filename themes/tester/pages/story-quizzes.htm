How to Use the Story Quizzes

  1. Read the story carefully - Pay attention to details, characters, and events.
  2. Use the highlighting tool - Click the "Highlight Mode" button to activate, then:
    • On desktop: Select text with your mouse to highlight important information
    • On mobile: Touch and drag your finger across text to draw highlight marks
  3. Answer the questions - Test your comprehension with multiple-choice questions based on the story.
  4. Review your results - See your score and learn from any mistakes.

These interactive quizzes are designed to improve your reading comprehension and attention to detail - skills that are essential for debugging and problem-solving in the tech world!
